Install Apple Car Play

So, you've just bought a car and you're looking to equip it with Apple Car Play? Here's what you need to know.

First of all, you'll need an iPhone 5 or later to use Apple Car Play. Your car will also need to be compatible with the feature. You can find a list of compatible cars on the Apple website.

Once you've determined that your car is compatible, you'll need to download the Apple Car Play app from the App Store. Then, connect your iPhone to your car's USB port. Your phone will automatically start charging, and the Car Play app will launch.

From there, it's pretty simple to use. Just tap the Car Play icon on your dashboard and navigate through your music, podcasts, navigation, and more.
